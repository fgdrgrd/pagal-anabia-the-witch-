Pagal Anabia: The Witch

A dark tale of magic, mystery, and the curse of the forgotten.

Deep within the silent forest, where moonlight barely touched the ground, lived a witch named Pagal Anabia. Her laughter echoed through the trees — not with joy, but with a secret that time itself feared.

Villagers spoke of her eyes — glowing like dying embers — and her power to twist reality with a single whisper. Some said she was cursed, others believed she had made a deal with something far darker than the night.

One stormy night, the wind carried screams from the forest. Anabia had begun her final ritual — one that would tear open the veil between worlds. Thunder cracked, and the earth trembled as shadows danced around her.

When the lightning struck, the forest fell silent. Pagal Anabia was gone — only her wand, burning with a cold blue flame, remained.

Her final words echoed in the darkness:
“Gagar will come back...”

No one knows who — or what — Gagar is. But every few decades, when the moon turns crimson, a strange wind blows through the same forest... and whispers her name again.
